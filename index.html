Currency link exchange

CLINK short for Currency Link Exchange, our plan is to create a finance commerce app for both Mobile and Web. The mission is simple; make it available to everyone with or without bank accounts, P2P/P2B/B2B currency exchange/transfer worldwide with favorable exchange rates and low fees.

To make it simple, CLINK is a service that connects all the world currencies with service such as money exchange, payments and last but not the least remittance.
